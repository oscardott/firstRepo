College Experience

My name is Oscar, and my experience in college so far has been good. I'm happy to be in the communication design major after struggling to decide what I wanted to pursue. Discovering the path of illustration at this school has really inspired me. I've gotten passing grades, and I feel that my creative skills are developing, which I am grateful for. Overall, my college experience is going well, and I hope it stays that way.

A quote that I like is an excerpt from a poem written in 1717 by Alexander Pope. I learned of it from the film Eternal Sunshine of the Spotless Mind, which is one that I really enjoyed.

How happy is the blameless vestal’s lot!
The world forgetting, by the world forgot.
Eternal sunshine of the spotless mind!
Each pray’r accepted, and each wish resign’d.

—Alexander Pope, Eloisa to Abelard
